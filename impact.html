Diplomacy & delegations

Connecting Nepal with the world

As founder and local lead, I've designed and hosted two Oxford Diplomatic Society delegations to Nepal (2025 & 2026) and represented Nepal on delegations abroad, opening doors from government ministries to rural communities.

April 2025

Oxford Diplomatic Society's first delegation to Nepal

Served as local lead for the Society's inaugural delegation, bringing Oxford and Cambridge students and alumni to meet ministers, the EU and British ambassadors, and UN bodies, and signed a Memorandum of Understanding between the ODS and IRD.

April 2026

Second Oxford delegation to Nepal

Led a follow-up of 15 delegates from Oxford, LSE, Princeton, Dartmouth, Harvard, Kenyon, and Greenwich across 27 engagements in ten days, in partnership with the Britain Nepal Society, from a dinner at the British Ambassador's residence to policy dialogues at PRI and IIDS and a dawn trek to Kalinchowk.

Building on the ground

Kapileshwar Mahadev Temple Hike

Developed and documented a heritage trekking trail with Panchakanya Rural Municipality, mapping seven cultural and ecological sites from the Tadi to the Likhu river to support sustainable and spiritual tourism.

An IRD program under the APT Young Professionals and Students Programme, in partnership with Nepal's Ministry of Information and Communication, teaching rural women farmers in Nuwakot to use social media to sell produce directly and cut out the middlemen.
